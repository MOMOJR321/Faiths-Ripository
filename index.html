About

Background and Education

I was born in a town called Choma, located in the southern part of Zambia. I spent most of my childhood in the area and my family lived there too. I have grown up in a Christian home and I am a dedicated Christian believer. I have one younger sibling and live in a family of four.

My early education as a child began in the year 2005 at a nursery school my family owns, a year later I switched to a nearby day school called St. Abraham daycare and spent another year completing my day school.

I then started my first grade at Faith-wood Christian school and learned for 7 years which was the completion of my junior education with various outstanding performances. In 2014 I was enrolled at Choma Secondary School to attend my high and senior education and spent five years at the institution where I attained various awards for exceptional performances. By the end of 2019, I completed high school and was ready for tertiary education (university).

It did not take much time as only 6 months into my gap year, three of the largest universities in Zambia requested that I join their institutions and take on certain different programs they had offered. Institutions of which included (The University of Zambia (UNZA), Mulungushi University, and The Copperbelt University (CBU)) and the decision was up to me to make.

I am currently in my 2nd year of study at The Copperbelt University pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering and expected to graduate in the year 2024.
